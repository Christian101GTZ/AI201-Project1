## Top 3 Picks
- **God of War Ragnarök (2022)** — A dark Norse saga with heavy combat upgrades and a father-son journey. A must-play action RPG.
- **Cyberpunk 2077 (2022)** — A neon open world built for endless choices; updates turned Night City into a great playground.
- **Baldur's Gate 3 (2023)** — Deep character options, tactical turn-based fights, decisions that change the story.

---

## 1. God of War Ragnarök — 10/10 — Action-Adventure RPG (2022, Santa Monica Studio)
Playtime ~25h main / ~60h completionist. Cinematic, single-shot camera; combat rewards strategy and reflexes; emotional Kratos/Atreus story. Generous accessibility settings. "The pinnacle of narrative-driven action RPGs." Best cinematic action RPG on PS5.

## 2. Cyberpunk 2077 — 9.8/10 — Open-World Action RPG (2022, CD Projekt Red)
Playtime ~25h main / ~100h completionist. A neon-lit sprawling Night City where choices matter; cybernetic implants and combat specialization. Satisfying combat for guns, stealth, or augments. Some bugs remain but patching made it really good. Best open-world RPG adventure for PS5.

## 3. Baldur's Gate 3 — 9.7/10 — Turn-Based RPG (2023, Larian Studios)
Playtime ~75h main / ~150h completionist. New standard for turn-based RPGs with unprecedented freedom; every decision marks the story. Party management, character creation, branching paths. Accessible to newcomers, deep for veterans. Best choice-driven PS5 RPG.

## 4. Horizon: Forbidden West — 9.5/10 — Action-Adventure RPG (2022, Guerrilla Games)
Playtime ~30h main / ~60h completionist. Exploration plus thrilling combat against colossal robotic beasts; Aloy's journey. Strategy/action balance; intuitive mechanics for casual players. Best open-world exploration on PS5.

## 5. Elden Ring — 9.4/10 — Action RPG / Soulslike (2022, FromSoftware)
Playtime ~50h main / ~130h completionist. Souls combat in a seamless open world; freedom to explore, no nagging quest markers. Builds for melee, magic, or hybrid. Brutally hard in places — part of the Souls challenge. Best PS5 Souls-like open-world RPG.

## 6. Like a Dragon: Infinite Wealth — 9.2/10 — Turn-Based JRPG (2024, Ryu ga Gotoku / SEGA)
Playtime ~60h main / ~100h completionist. Heart and hilarity; deep yet approachable turn-based combat; quirky side activities in Hawaii. Best PS5 modern JRPG. Recommended for newcomers to the genre.

## 7. Final Fantasy VII Rebirth — 9/10 — Action RPG (2024, Square Enix)
Playtime ~40h main / ~80h completionist. Expands the classic universe; hybrid combat merging fast action with tactical depth; team compositions. Some early-zone pacing issues. Best PS5 RPG reimagined classic.

## 8. Demon's Souls — 8.9/10 — Action RPG / Soulslike (2020, FromSoftware / Bluepoint)
Playtime ~15h main / ~30h completionist. Next-gen visual overhaul of a legendary RPG; punishing but rewarding combat; dark gothic atmosphere. High difficulty by design. Best punishing Souls-like RPG for PS5.

## 9. Final Fantasy XVI — 8.8/10 — Action RPG (2023, Square Enix)
Playtime ~25h main / ~60h completionist. Mature, politically charged narrative; fast-paced action combat with cinematic Eikon battles. Some may miss turn-based tactics. Best action-focused JRPG on PS5.

## 10. Diablo IV — 8.5/10 — Action RPG / Loot-based (2023, Blizzard)
Playtime ~20h main / ~70h completionist. Gothic open world full of dungeons and an amazing loot system; visceral combat for solo or online co-op. Flexible classes. **Supports co-op** (seamless multiplayer dungeon runs). Best PS5 dark loot-driven RPG.

## 11. The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt – Complete Edition — 8.5/10 — Action-Adventure RPG (2022 PS5, CD Projekt)
Playtime ~40h main / ~100h completionist. The gold standard for open-world RPGs; rich layered story; side quests that stand alone. Challenging but satisfying combat. Best story-driven open-world RPG on PS5. (Recommended for story-focused fans.)

## 12. Persona 5 Royal — 8.3/10 — Turn-Based JRPG / Social Sim (2022 PS5, Atlus / SEGA)
Playtime ~80h main / ~120h completionist. Blends daily life simulation with dungeon crawling; memorable characters; strategic combat needing thoughtful planning. Stylish visuals and unforgettable soundtrack. Best PS5 stylish turn-based RPG. (One of the longest playtimes on PS5.)

## 13. Disco Elysium – The Final Cut — 8.0/10 — Narrative RPG (2019, ZA/UM)
Playtime ~20h main / ~30h completionist. Removes traditional combat entirely; a revolutionary dialogue system where skills shape every interaction; politically charged murder mystery. Demands thought over action; deep replayability. Best PS5 narrative RPG.

## FAQ
- **Best RPG on PS5 right now?** God of War Ragnarök.
- **Is Elden Ring good on PS5?** Yes — stable performance, quick load times; one of the best open-world experiences on the system.
- **Longest playtime?** Persona 5 Royal and Baldur's Gate 3 (80–100+ hours).
- **Co-op RPGs on PS5?** Yes — Diablo IV (seamless multiplayer) and Baldur's Gate 3 (co-op campaign).
- **Can I play PS4 RPGs on PS5?** Yes, via backward compatibility; The Witcher 3 and Persona 5 Royal have upgraded versions.
