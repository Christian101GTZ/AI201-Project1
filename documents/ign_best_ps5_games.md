# The Best PS5 Games — IGN (Top 25, 2025)

Source: https://www.ign.com/articles/the-best-ps5-games
Type: Editorial ranking (IGN staff Face-Off vote)
Domain: PS5 Game Discovery Guide
Note: IGN's top 25 PS5 games, ranked by staff vote. Criteria limited to games with PS5-specific versions. Updated July 15, 2025.

---

## 1. Elden Ring — FromSoftware (Feb 25, 2022) — Open-World Action-RPG (Soulsborne)
A fantastic starting point for the soulsborne genre. Its streamlined introduction, versatility in accommodating any playstyle, and open-world design alleviate the "brick wall" of getting stuck on a boss. Full of godlike bosses, threateningly large maps, and intriguing NPCs. The Shadow of the Erdtree DLC adds a huge amount of high-quality content. One of the best-reviewed games in modern history; Game of the Year 2022 (IGN and The Game Awards).

## 2. Baldur's Gate 3 — Larian Studios (Sep 6, 2023) — RPG
A massive roleplaying adventure, enormously ambitious yet endearingly old-school. Epic storytelling with memorable, detailed characters and a multitude of meaningful choices. Tactical turn-based combat loyal to its Dungeons & Dragons roots — intimidating to get into, but offers over 100 hours of "all-thriller, no-filler" questing. 2023 Game of the Year nominee.

## 3. Metaphor: ReFantazio — Studio Zero / Atlus (Oct 11, 2024) — Fantasy JRPG
More than "fantasy Persona" — a culmination of Atlus' RPGs blending social-sim elements and dungeon-crawling combat in a brand-new fantasy world with a mature story paralleling real political realities. The Job-like Archetype system offers deep party building. Distinct Atlus style and music; an "all-timer RPG" (~80 hours).

## 4. God of War Ragnarok — Santa Monica Studio (Nov 8, 2022) — Action-Adventure
"One of the easiest games to recommend to anyone asking what to play first on their PS5." A satisfying combination of combat, puzzle-solving, and exploration wrapped in a heartwarming story of family and letting go. Fantastic soundtrack and jaw-dropping landscapes. A benchmark for what PlayStation exclusives can do on PS5.

## 5. Final Fantasy VII Rebirth — Square Enix (Feb 29, 2024) — Action-RPG
The middle chapter of the FFVII remake trilogy. A grand sense of adventure in a beautiful recreation of the original's world; each character grows over a 70–80 hour journey. Adds tactical layers (Synergy abilities, partner attacks) on top of Remake's action-RPG formula. "One of the year's best RPGs."

## 6. Astro Bot — Team Asobi (Sep 6, 2024) — Platformer (PS5 exclusive)
Goes toe-to-toe with Super Mario Odyssey, taking players through hyper-colorful worlds full of imaginative level design and loving references to PlayStation history. Each world introduces a surprising new ability, and few games make better use of the DualSense controller.

## 7. Persona 5 Royal — P-Studio / Atlus (Oct 21, 2022) — JRPG
Long the benchmark for best JRPG of all time. Endlessly stylish art and a jazzy soundtrack, iconic characters, and a thought-provoking story. Turn-based, small-party combat at its best. Royal makes nearly everything bigger and better than the 2016 original — almost like getting another entire game on top.

## 8. Final Fantasy VII Remake / Intergrade — Square Enix (Apr 10, 2020) — Action-RPG
The first chapter of the FFVII remake in crisp 4K, fully voice-acted. Swaps turn-based battles for faster real-time action (with a classic-mode option). Expanded side stories around Midgar. The Intermission DLC stars Yuffie with the most engaging combat. Leads into FFVII Rebirth.

## 9. The Last of Us Part 1 — Naughty Dog (Sep 2, 2022) — Action-Adventure (story-driven)
The best way to experience the 2013 classic. Improved character models and facial animation make the phenomenal acting more meaningful; DualSense haptics greatly improve immersion. Incredible, emotional story. (No multiplayer mode.)

## 10. The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt — Complete Edition — CD Projekt Red (Dec 14, 2022) — Open-World RPG
One of the best RPGs of the last generation. The Complete Edition's current-gen updates (smooth frame rate, 4K textures) fix prior technical shortcomings, making this the definitive version of one of the best RPGs of all time.

## 11. Hades — Supergiant Games (Aug 13, 2021) — Roguelite
"The current gold standard of the roguelite genre, and it isn't even close." Exhilarating combat, incredible soundtrack, clever well-written story. Never feels punishing in defeat — dying is part of the fun and comes with rewards. A masterclass of roguelite design.

## 12. Grand Theft Auto V / GTA Online — Rockstar North (Mar 15, 2022) — Open-World Action
Its sprawling, meticulously detailed map remains the high bar most open-world games aspire to. Dense with content: driving, shooting, a three-protagonist story, side activities, and the bustling Grand Theft Auto Online with creative tools and mods.

## 13. The Last of Us Part 2 Remastered — Naughty Dog (Jan 19, 2024) — Action-Adventure (story-driven)
The definitive way to experience the ambitious, sweeping sequel — an impeccably assembled stealth-action game with a heavy spotlight on revenge and self-destruction. Includes a new mode to play the non-linear story in chronological order.

## 14. Marvel's Spider-Man 2 — Insomniac Games (Oct 20, 2023) — Action-Adventure (superhero)
A showcase for the PS5. Its two–Spider-Men story brings comic-book battles with Venom and Kraven to life, balancing humor and heavier themes. Refined combat and web-swinging, blockbuster action moments, near-instant load times, and best-in-class fast travel.

## 15. Cyberpunk 2077 — CD Projekt Red (Feb 15, 2022) — Open-World RPG
A spectacular recovery after its rough launch. The 2.0 patch and Phantom Liberty expansion delivered years of polish to combat and skill systems. 30+ hours of base story plus ~20 in the spy-themed Phantom Liberty (starring Idris Elba). Exploring Night City stands apart from anything else.

## 16. Resident Evil 4 — Capcom (Mar 24, 2023) — Survival Horror
A ground-up remake of the 2005 action-horror classic, modernized to thrill present-day gamers. Builds tension with high-intensity combat against a ferocious cast of creatures and rich detail. Many games have evoked RE4's style, but none nailed it as well.

## 17. Alan Wake 2 — Remedy Entertainment (Oct 27, 2023) — Survival Horror
A standout single-player adventure shifting from slow-burn psychological terror to frantic survival-horror action, with immaculate art direction and audio design. "Bloody, bonkers, and for the most part utterly brilliant" — unlike any other adventure on PS5.

## 18. Ghost of Tsushima Director's Cut — Sucker Punch (Jul 17, 2020) — Open-World Action-Adventure
The definitive version of a must-play PlayStation game. Stunning graphics and map; great DualSense use in exploration and combat, Japanese lip-sync, and visual/performance improvements. Sequel Ghost of Yotei is in the works.

## 19. Balatro — LocalThunk (Feb 20, 2024) — Deckbuilding Roguelite
A poker-themed deckbuilding roguelite that's easy to learn but endlessly deep as you discover how joker cards synergize to maximize score multipliers. Its pixelated 2D graphics set it apart; you may sink more hours into it than the biggest RPGs.

## 20. Like a Dragon: Infinite Wealth — Ryu Ga Gotoku Studio (Jan 25, 2024) — Turn-Based RPG
A collection of minigames inside larger activities, wrapped in a life sim, inside a 50-hour turn-based RPG — and it all works. Much of the story takes place in Hawaii. Honors the Yakuza series' roots while delivering one of its most emotional, best legacy stories.

## 21. Star Wars Jedi: Survivor — Respawn Entertainment (Apr 28, 2023) — Action-Adventure
Vaults over the original. Control a full-fledged Jedi Knight Cal Kestis with superhuman mobility and multiple lightsaber styles in fantastic, challenging combat. Larger, denser worlds and a memorable cast; does virtually everything better than its predecessor.

## 22. Horizon Forbidden West — Guerrilla Games (Feb 18, 2022) — Open-World Action-RPG
Builds on Horizon Zero Dawn with a deeper story, explosive fights against robotic foes, and blockbuster sequences, plus lively settlements and thoughtful sidequests. Gorgeous on PS5 with a genuine sense of exploration — a fantastic showcase of PS5's power.

## 23. Final Fantasy XVI — Square Enix CBU III (Jun 22, 2023) — Action-RPG
A dramatic departure moving the series toward pure action, without losing its excellently told, dark, captivating tale and memorable characters. Best-in-class combat among action-RPGs, paired with an unbelievable soundtrack. Proves action games can be 50+ hour epics.

## 24. Returnal — Housemarque (Apr 30, 2021) — Roguelike Bullet-Hell Shooter
A trailblazer combining Housemarque's intense arcadey bullet-hell expertise with deep roguelite mechanics and a dense, chilling sci-fi atmosphere. The early inability to suspend long runs was patched; now hard to find flaws in this bold, ambitious experience.

## 25. Devil May Cry 5 — Capcom (Mar 2019) — Character Action
"There's still nothing quite like a Devil May Cry game." One of the best character action games ever made, offering unmatched creativity and expression through combat across Nero, V, and Dante. Near-bottomless depth with finely tuned difficulty options.

## IGN's FAQ

**What is the best game on PS5?** It depends on your taste, but IGN recommends Elden Ring. For a PlayStation exclusive you can't play anywhere else, God of War: Ragnarok is a very good option.

**What are the best games on PS Plus (Extra/Premium)?** A few of the best include Assassin's Creed Valhalla, Bloodborne, Ghost of Tsushima, God of War, Horizon Zero Dawn, Marvel's Spider-Man, and Red Dead Redemption 2.
