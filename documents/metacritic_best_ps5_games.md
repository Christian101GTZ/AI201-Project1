# Best PS5 Games — Metacritic

Source: https://www.metacritic.com/browse/game/ps5/
Type: Aggregate critic-score ranking
Domain: PS5 Game Discovery Guide
Note: Top 24 highest-Metascore PS5 games (of ~1,105 catalogued).

---

**1. Elden Ring** — Metascore 96 — Feb 25, 2022 — M — Fantasy Action-RPG
A collaboration between FromSoftware and George R.R. Martin, this expansive fantasy adventure features challenging combat, exploration of a massive open world, and encounters with diverse enemies from supernatural creatures to mythological beings.

**2. Baldur's Gate 3** — Metascore 96 — Sep 6, 2023 — M — RPG
Players navigate the Forgotten Realms facing an ancient evil threatening Baldur's Gate. The narrative involves managing a mind flayer parasite and making consequential choices affecting party dynamics and world outcomes.

**3. Astro Bot** — Metascore 94 — Sep 6, 2024 — E10+ — Platformer
After the PS5 mothership is attacked, Astro must rescue the scattered crew. Features innovative power-ups tailored for PS5's DualSense controller, emphasizing immersion and PlayStation character crossovers.

**4. Elden Ring: Shadow of the Erdtree** — Metascore 94 — Jun 21, 2024 — M — Fantasy Action-RPG Expansion
A major expansion introducing new pathways and adventures within the Lands Between.

**5. The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt - Complete Edition** — Metascore 94 — Dec 14, 2022 — M — Open-World Action-RPG
Players assume the role of Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter, traversing a war-torn continent exceeding 100 hours of gameplay. The Complete Edition includes the base game, two story expansions, and PS5 enhancements featuring ray tracing and haptic feedback support.

**6. Metaphor: ReFantazio** — Metascore 94 — Oct 11, 2024 — T — Fantasy JRPG
From Persona's creative team, this fantasy narrative follows companions on a journey to lift a curse from a kingdom's prince. Features dynamic job classes called "Archetypes," time management mechanics, and a competitive Royal Tournament storyline.

**7. God of War: Ragnarok** — Metascore 94 — Nov 9, 2022 — M — Action-Adventure
Kratos and Atreus confront Fimbulwinter and prepare for Ragnarok across nine Norse realms. The sequel introduces newly accessible locations like Vanaheim and Asgard, featuring diverse environments from otherworldly jungles to subterranean networks.

**8. Hades II** — Metascore 94 — Apr 14, 2026 — T — Action Roguelike
As Melinoe, the Underworld's princess, players battle the Titan of Time using dark sorcery. Features expanded mythology, new locations, upgraded progression systems, and continuously evolving storytelling.

**9. Hades** — Metascore 93 — Aug 13, 2021 — T — Roguelike Dungeon Crawler
As the Underworld's immortal prince, players wield Olympian powers while attempting escape. Combines fast-paced action with fully-voiced characters, thousands of viable character builds through divine boons, and structural design enabling permanent progression across repeated playthroughs.

**10. Tetris Effect: Connected** — Metascore 93 — Feb 22, 2023 — E — Puzzle
A visually spectacular Tetris reimagining where game elements synchronize with music and gameplay. Offers 30+ stages, 10+ modes, and multiplayer expansion with cooperative and competitive online/local options.

**11. Resident Evil 4** — Metascore 93 — Mar 24, 2023 — M — Survival Horror
A ground-up remake of the 2005 classic modernizing graphics, controls, and storytelling. Agent Leon Kennedy investigates a European village regarding a missing presidential daughter, encountering unprecedented threats.

**12. Clair Obscur: Expedition 33** — Metascore 92 — Apr 24, 2025 — M — Turn-Based RPG
A fantasy narrative set in Belle Époque-inspired landscapes where Expedition 33 battles a cursed Paintress. Combines turn-based mechanics with real-time combat elements, featuring customizable character builds and strategic party composition.

**13. Hollow Knight: Silksong** — Metascore 92 — Sep 4, 2025 — Metroidvania
Players control Hornet, princess-protector of Hallownest, navigating an unfamiliar silk-ruled kingdom. The sequel features new powers, extensive exploration, challenging boss encounters, and ancient mystery revelations.

**14. Final Fantasy VII Rebirth** — Metascore 92 — Feb 29, 2024 — T — Action-RPG
The second installment in the Final Fantasy VII remake trilogy following Cloud's party beyond Midgar. Features an open-world structure with mysterious exploration and consequences determining planetary fate.

**15. Forza Horizon 5** — Metascore 92 — Apr 29, 2025 — E — Racing
Players explore dynamically evolving Mexican landscapes with unlimited driving action across hundreds of vehicles representing global automotive excellence.

**16. Street Fighter 6** — Metascore 92 — Jun 2, 2023 — T — Fighting
Powered by Capcom's RE ENGINE, featuring 18 fighters with redesigned appearances and cinematic specials. Offers three control schemes (Classic, Modern, Dynamic), real-time commentary, and a Drive Gauge resource management system.

**17. Demon's Souls** — Metascore 92 — Nov 11, 2020 — M — Action-RPG
A complete remake by Bluepoint Games of the PlayStation classic. Players face supernatural horrors in a fog-laden kingdom, with enhanced visuals and performance optimized for current-generation hardware.

**18. Mina the Hollower** — Metascore 91 — May 29, 2026 — E10+ — Action-Adventure
From Shovel Knight's developers, this pixel-art adventure follows Mina on a cursed island rescue mission. Features burrowing mechanics, whip-based combat, extensive weapon/trinket customization, and challenging boss encounters.

**19. Slay The Princess - The Pristine Cut** — Metascore 91 — Oct 24, 2024 — M — Interactive Horror Narrative
A fully voice-acted horror tragicomedy with hand-illustrated frames. Player dialogue and beliefs dramatically shift narrative paths, featuring multiple unique princesses and extensive content additions in the Pristine Cut version.

**20. Split Fiction** — Metascore 91 — Mar 6, 2025 — T — Co-op Action-Adventure
From It Takes Two's studio, two contrasting writers become trapped within their stories—one sci-fi, one fantasy. Gameplay alternates between genres with unexpected moments and cooperative puzzle-solving emphasizing partnership.

**21. Persona 5 Royal** — Metascore 91 — Oct 21, 2022 — M — JRPG
Students moonlight as "Phantom Thieves," infiltrating corrupted minds to enforce moral rehabilitation. Features expanded confidants, new story arcs, alternate endings, grappling hook mechanics, and distinctive visual presentation.

**22. Dave the Diver** — Metascore 91 — Apr 16, 2024 — E10+ — Adventure
A marine exploration adventure set in the mysterious Blue Hole. By day, players dive; by night, they operate a sushi restaurant, gradually uncovering deep-sea mysteries.

**23. Rogue Legacy 2** — Metascore 90 — Jun 20, 2023 — E10+ — Roguelike Platformer
A genealogical roguelike combining randomized runs with persistent family progression. Each heir possesses unique traits and abilities; parents' accumulated wealth upgrades the family manor, improving successors' viability.

**24. Citizen Sleeper 2: Starward Vector** — Metascore 90 — Jan 31, 2025 — T — Adventure/Narrative
An android sleeper escapes corporate ownership with a malfunctioning body and stolen ship. Players maintain and upgrade their vessel while recruiting crew members throughout a space-exploration narrative focused on refuge-building.
