## Unmissable PlayStation Studios Games

**SAROS** (Release: April 30, 2026) — Sci-fi shooter where players "rise against the corrupting cycle of the eclipse and battle behemothic alien bosses" on planet Carcosa. From Returnal creators.

**Marathon** (Release: March 5, 2026) — Competitive multiplayer set on Tau Ceti IV. "Rival factions hire Runners to compete for the resources left behind" from a derelict ship.

**Ghost of Yotei** (Release: October 2, 2025) — Historical action-adventure. "In 1603, Atsu sets out on a journey in the lands surrounding Mount Yotei" amid grasslands and snowy terrain.

**God of War: Sons of Sparta** — Prequel exploring Kratos' Spartan training with his brother Deimos.

**Stellar Blade** — Story-driven action game in a "post-apocalyptic world that blends beauty and horror."

**God of War Ragnarök** — "Fimbulwinter is well underway. Kratos and Atreus must journey to each of the Nine Realms in search of answers."

**Gran Turismo 7** — Racing sim with a "staggering collection of game modes" for competitive racers, collectors, and photographers.

**Lost Soul Aside** — Action adventure across "snow-covered vistas, long-lost ruins and ancient alien relics."

**Ratchet & Clank: Rift Apart** — "Dimension-hopping" adventure fighting an evil emperor from another reality.

**Marvel's Spider-Man 2** — Team-up with Peter Parker and Miles Morales in a new adventure for PS5.

**Astro Bot** — Platformer where players "board ASTRO's trusty Dual Speeder" through spectacular worlds.

**Helldivers 2** — Co-op shooter for up to four players fighting "an alien scourge threatening" Earth's safety.

**Death Stranding 2: On the Beach** — Sequel where "Sam and his allies return to save humanity from extinction."
