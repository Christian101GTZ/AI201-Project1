# Best Online Multiplayer Games on PS5 — Push Square (Top 40, community-voted)

Source: https://www.pushsquare.com/guides/best-online-multiplayer-games-on-ps5
Type: Editorial/community genre ranking (Online Multiplayer / Co-op)
Domain: PS5 Game Discovery Guide
Note: Community-voted ranking of the best online multiplayer (competitive and cooperative) games on PS5. Covers shooters, racing, sports, fighting, action RPGs, party, and co-op adventures. "Available On: PS+ Extra" noted where applicable.

---

## 1. Ghost of Tsushima: Director's Cut — Sucker Punch (2021) — 9.1 — PS+ Extra
One of the greatest open-world games of the PS4 era on PS5, with the Iki Island expansion. Aggressive samurai combat, wind-guided exploration, stunning DualSense support. The robust online co-op suite **Ghost of Tsushima: Legends** rounds out the package.

## 2. Baldur's Gate 3 — Larian Studios (2023) — 9.0
A role-playing masterclass — deeply engaging, with unrivaled depth and player freedom, fantastic characters, and superb storytelling. Online/local co-op campaign. A defining example of its genre.

## 3. Gran Turismo 7 — Polyphony Digital (2022) — 8.9
Returns to the RPG-format sim racing of the series. Slick driving model, hundreds of cars, the addictive GT Café, and the competitive online approach from GT Sport. The most complete Gran Turismo in a generation; playable in PSVR2.

## 4. Demon's Souls — Bluepoint Games (2020) — 8.9 — PS+ Extra
Bluepoint's remarkable remake of the legendary action RPG that spawned the Soulsborne genre. Uses original PS3 code for authenticity with best-in-class PS5 visuals, outstanding score, and drastically reduced load times.

## 5. Death Stranding 2: On the Beach — Kojima Productions (2025) — 8.8
A cinematic achievement — bigger and better than the original, with vastly improved combat and stealth alongside the cargo-delivery loop. A new visual benchmark on PS5 and a crown jewel of the library.

## 6. Elden Ring — FromSoftware (2022) — 8.7
FromSoftware's open-world magnum opus brings the Souls loop to the masses: a sandbox bursting with secrets, a complex combat system enabling varied playstyles, and devilish encounters. Supports online co-op.

## 7. Returnal — Housemarque (2021) — 8.6 — PS+ Extra
A frantic, ultra-tight third-person shooter with roguelike elements. Mysterious story melded with the loop structure, plus **online co-op**, daily challenges, and the replayable Tower of Sisyphus. Best-in-class arcade gameplay.

## 8. It Takes Two — Hazelight (2021) — 8.6 — PS+ Extra
The co-op game that springs to mind first. An estranged couple turned into dolls go on a wildly imaginative, endlessly varied adventure. Constant new puzzles and ideas; playable locally or online. **Pure co-op.**

## 9. Split Fiction — Hazelight (2025) — 8.6 (10/10 review)
Follow-up to It Takes Two — a co-op action adventure where two writers get stuck in their own sci-fi and fantasy stories. Unparalleled gameplay variety and inventive co-op design. "Arguably the best co-op experience on PS5." **Pure co-op.**

## 10. Tetris Effect — Enhance Games (2023) — 8.5
Classic falling-block Tetris merged with mesmerising visuals and entrancing music; sings in 4K/HDR and PSVR2. Many modes — the ultimate Tetris on PlayStation.

## 11. Synth Riders — Kluge Interactive (2023) — 8.5 (PSVR2)
A fantastic VR rhythm game with ~60 tunes; PSVR2 brings sharper graphics, better tracking, and haptics. A must-play for rhythm fans.

## 12. Death Stranding Director's Cut — Kojima Productions (2021) — 8.4 — PS+ Extra
The unique "strand game" enhanced for PS5: traverse a desolate landscape making deliveries, with every step a risk. Improved graphics/performance, DualSense, 3D audio, and new content.

## 13. Helldivers 2 — Arrowhead (2024) — 8.4
Live service done effortlessly — gameplay ahead of business models. Emergent cinematic moments arise from co-operative action and physics. Superb gunplay, phenomenal sound. **A blast with friends (co-op).**

## 14. Street Fighter 6 — Capcom (2023) — 8.3
Puts the series back on the map — the most robust package yet for competitive and casual players. New mechanics, accessible control schemes, smooth online play, and the high-effort World Tour mode. One of the best fighting games in recent memory.

## 15. Pavlov VR — Vankrupt (2023) — 8.2
One of the better VR multiplayer shooters: a wide variety of weapons and entertaining modes, intuitive to play, and a great laugh with friends.

## 16. The Nioh Collection — Team Ninja (2021) — 8.1
Both punishing-but-satisfying Nioh action RPGs (Japanese history/folklore) in one package, up to 120fps with DualSense support. Fast, deadly stance-swapping melee combat.

## 17. DOOM Eternal — id Software (2021) — 8.1 — PS+ Extra
The peak of fast, demanding, bloody FPS — a constantly-moving aggressive gameplay loop. Three visual modes (incl. ray tracing) and DualSense support on PS5.

## 18. Final Fantasy XIV Online: A Realm Reborn — Square Enix (2021) — 8.1
A near-failed MMO reborn into one of the greatest MMORPGs ever made. Bolstered by acclaimed expansions; many call it the best Final Fantasy of the last decade.

## 19. Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 1 + 2 — Vicarious Visions (2021) — 8.1
A brilliant remake of two impactful skateboarding classics, updated to modern standards with old-school arcade magic. Smooth performance, 4K, reduced loading. Pure joy for fans.

## 20. Granblue Fantasy: Relink — Cygames (2024) — 8.0 — PS+ Extra
A well-executed action RPG with hectic combat and outstanding boss battles; teamwork rewarded in co-op. An all-killer-no-filler 20-hour campaign and addictive grind.

## 21. Warhammer 40,000: Space Marine 2 — Saber Interactive (2024) — 8.0 — PS+ Extra
A gory third-person action shooter — a robust 40k adaptation with a campaign playable in **co-op**, online co-op missions, and competitive multiplayer. Jaw-dropping enemy counts and visceral combat.

## 22. Diablo 4 — Blizzard (2023) — 7.9
A top-down dungeon crawler about building your character with randomly generated loot and **teaming up with pals for co-op**. A huge world, gratifying progression, deep customisation.

## 23. Sackboy: A Big Adventure — Sumo Digital (2020) — 7.9 — PS+ Extra
A robust co-operative platformer with memorable musical moments and a cutesy DIY aesthetic. Fun for the whole family — easy to play, hard to master.

## 24. Mortal Kombat 11 Ultimate — NetherRealm (2020) — 7.8
A brawler with huge scope: ever-rotating towers, a sprawling single-player campaign, and an RPG-inspired loot system to tailor characters. A damn good time.

## 25. ARC Raiders — Embark (2025) — 7.8 (9/10 review)
A phenomenal extraction shooter — every run is different and your squad comes out with stories. One of the most accessible in the genre and respectful of your time. "One of the best multiplayer games this generation."

## 26. F1 2021 — Codemasters (2021) — 7.7
The most complete annual F1 sim: all teams/cars/tracks, an engrossing career mode, the Braking Point story mode, and Two Player Career. Excellent handling model.

## 27. No Man's Sky — Hello Games (2020) — 7.6
Procedurally generated space exploration fleshed out by huge updates; 18 quintillion planets. Best console version on PS5 — minimised load times, DualSense, optional PSVR2. Supports online multiplayer.

## 28. Overcooked: All You Can Eat — Ghost Town Games (2020) — 7.6 — PS+ Extra
Overcooked 1 & 2 remastered with online play and extra DLC. Forces you to truly work together for three-star ratings. "One of the best co-op party games in recent memory." **Pure co-op party.**

## 29. Dead Island 2 — Dambuster Studios (2023) — 7.6 — PS+ Extra
A great zombie-slaying adventure set in LA with gloriously gory action across dense open levels and a wide variety of weapons. Co-op zombie smashing.

## 30. Fall Guys — Mediatonic (2022) — 7.5
A madcap battle-royale platformer: up to 60 jellybean players compete in chaotic obstacle courses by elimination. Hilarious solo or with friends, for all ages — and free-to-play.

## 31. Destiny 2 — Bungie (2020) — 7.4 — PS+ Extra
Still top of the pile for gunplay. Campaigns, **co-op missions**, and competitive multiplayer, held together by excellent action. Tough for newcomers; best content behind paywalls.

## 32. Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty — Team Ninja (2023) — 7.4 — PS+ Extra
A Soulslike action RPG with a heavier emphasis on parrying and poise. Loosely based on Romance of the Three Kingdoms; team up with legendary warriors. Deep combat and thrilling boss battles.

## 33. Dragon Ball: Sparking! Zero — Spike Chunsoft (2024) — 7.4
The Budokai Tenkaichi series back in all but name — hundreds of characters in over-the-top arena brawls. A feature-packed, satisfying power-fantasy arena fighter.

## 34. WRC 9 — Kylotonn (2020) — 7.4
A rock-solid licensed rally sim with real-world routes/vehicles, deep career mode, excellent DualSense haptics, split-screen local multiplayer, and online championships. 60fps at 4K.

## 35. Wreckfest — Bugbear (2021) — 7.4
A modern reimagining of Destruction Derby's demolition racing at 60fps; far more responsive than its PS4 predecessor, with regular content updates.

## 36. The Dark Pictures Anthology: House of Ashes — Supermassive (2021) — 7.4
The closest the Dark Pictures Anthology has come to Until Dawn. Control multiple characters facing ancient monsters under Iraq's desert. "Get some friends over for a whale of a time." Co-op horror.

## 37. Deathloop — Arkane (2021) — 7.3
An immersive-sim assassin in a time loop must eliminate eight targets in one cycle. Fun powers, satisfying gunplay — "a supercharged Dishonored." Player invasions add tension; great 60s aesthetic.

## 38. Sniper Elite 5 — Rebellion (2022) — 7.2 — PS+ Extra
Stealth and long-range sniping in occupied France, with dense vertical levels and sublime gunplay. The Axis Invasion mechanic lets players infiltrate your campaign. Deeply replayable.

## 39. Hot Wheels Unleashed — Milestone (2021) — 7.2
A colorful arcade racer on the famous orange track. Excellent handling, treacherous courses, split-screen and online multiplayer, a track editor, and hundreds of cars to collect.

## 40. Borderlands 3 — Gearbox (2020) — 7.2
A looter-shooter that looks and plays better than ever on PS5 (up to 120fps). Tons of quest and gun variety with signature humor. "Few better co-op shooters — a blast with friends, local or online."
